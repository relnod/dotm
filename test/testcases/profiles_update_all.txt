Multiple profiles to be updated via all
---
/home/$USER/.dotfiles/dotm.toml#[profiles.1]\npath="/dotfiles1"\n[profiles.2]\npath="/dotfiles2"

/dotfiles1/tmux/.tmux.conf
/dotfiles2/bash/.bashrc
---
dotm update all
---
/home/$USER/.tmux.conf:ln
/home/$USER/.bashrc:ln
===
