Executes post update hook from hooks.toml
---
/home/$USER/.dotfiles/dotm.toml#[profiles.default]\npath="/dotfiles"
/dotfiles/tmux/hooks.toml#post_update=["touch $BASE/a"]
/dotfiles/tmux/.tmux.conf
---
dotm update default
---
/home/$USER/.tmux.conf:ln
/a
===
Top level hooks respects includes
---
/home/$USER/.dotfiles/dotm.toml#[profiles.default]\npath="/dotfiles"\nincludes=["a"]
/dotfiles/tmux/hooks.toml#post_update=["touch $BASE/a"]
/dotfiles/tmux/.tmux.conf
---
dotm update default
---
/a:deleted
===
Top level hooks respects excludes
---
/home/$USER/.dotfiles/dotm.toml#[profiles.default]\npath="/dotfiles"\nexcludes=["tmux"]
/dotfiles/tmux/hooks.toml#post_update=["touch $BASE/a"]
/dotfiles/tmux/.tmux.conf
---
dotm update default
---
/a:deleted
